ABOUT US

    Innovative Auto Components Pvt. Ltd is involved in manufacturing high quality precision auto components and assemblies for the automotive industry. Since our inception, our focus in everything that we do has been to break the traditional barriers. We challenge conventional thinking at every step and make innovative use of our resources to create value for our stakeholders and customers alike.
    Our extensive product portfolio consists of a range of automotive products. Our expert team utilizes Lean manufacturing techniques, TPM, Six Sigma, Value Stream Mapping, Kaizen and Poka-Yoke to ensure superior product quality and delivery.
